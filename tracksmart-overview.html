The Opportunity

Racing faces a generational challenge: attracting and retaining younger fans. The missing ingredient is not better marketing — it is a better product experience. Today's sports fan expects instant, intelligent, data-driven insight. TrackSmart AI delivers exactly that, built for Thoroughbred and Standardbred racing, on tracks across North America and internationally.

Imagine a fan clicking any race on Equibase.com and watching AI generate a real-time race report — pace projections, speed figures, trip notes, and race flow analysis — in seconds. That is TrackSmart AI.

What Is TrackSmart AI

TrackSmart AI is a working, tested AI handicapping engine that parses Thoroughbred and Standardbred racing data — domestic and international — and produces structured race analysis reports with consistent reliability. It is not a concept or a prototype. It produces results today.

Built to Think, Not Just Calculate

Run the same race through TrackSmart AI five times and the top contenders stay consistent — but the exact order can shift. That is not noise. That is exactly what happens when five world-class handicappers study the same race independently: they tend to agree on who belongs, and disagree on the order.

Current Capabilities

  • Reliably parses past performance PDFs into structured data — models built for DRF, Brisnet, Equibase, and TrackMaster (harness)
  • Generates full race reports including pace projections and race flow
  • Speed figure analysis and trip note integration
  • Identifies pace scenarios: wire-to-wire, stalker, closer setups
  • Grounded in structured handicapping logic, not freeform generation
  • Validated across 3,400+ real races (Thoroughbred & Standardbred) at an 80%+ Top 4 strike rate
  • Built and tested across domestic and international racing jurisdictions

Why This, Why Now

The Jockey Club has publicly committed to fan engagement and growing the sport with younger audiences. TrackSmart AI is built for exactly that mission. The technology exists. The data infrastructure exists at Equibase. What has been missing is the AI layer that connects them into a consumer product.

TrackSmart AI bridges that gap — today, with a working system across two breeds and both domestic and international cards — not a roadmap.

The Vision

25,000 fans at Keeneland. 25,000 phones in their pockets.

The plan has always been an iOS app. A fan walks into Keeneland, opens TrackSmart AI, taps the upcoming race, and watches the AI handicap it live — rankings, pace shape, and the reasoning behind them, delivered in under two minutes.

Their friend two seats over runs the same race and gets a slightly different order on the same short list of contenders. That is not a bug — it is what makes the experience feel real. It is the same conversation that happens in the grandstand every single day, just generated instantly and explained clearly, for a fan who may have never picked up a Form before.
